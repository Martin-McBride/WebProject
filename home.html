Apostrophe Rant

I really don't like apostrophes, but I feel like the writing here sounds a bit awkward, so I've decided to just give in and start using them anyway. As for why I hate apostrophes, I really don't know, it all started when I was just a boy. I was new and I was green and I didn't know any better.

Anyway, enjoy the website. I worked on it to some degree of difficulty, though it didn't take very long cuz it's a pretty simple website. It's just some basic html and css magic thrown in. Luckily this isn't graded on content or appearance and more just on functionality and structure.

My name is Martin McBride. I am a computer science major at Siena College. I am here to tell you about some things. What are those things? I do not know yet, but I am sure we will find out soon. I guess we will start with why I made this website, which is a good question with a pretty simple answer. This is basically just to show off some HTML and CSS stuff, and not much else.

If you read the aside before reading this for some reason, you'll have heard my whole apostrophe speech already. But the truth is, I wrote this paragraph after I wrote the aside.
